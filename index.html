About Me

Hi, I'm Becky Rush, a Brighton based web developer available for work!

I am an inquisitive, hardworking and creative individual with a strong interest in digital media, both design and production.

I study Digital Media Development at the University of Brighton, developing my skills across a variety of computing disciplines.

I specialise in Front End Web Development, tinkering with HTML, CSS and JavaScript to produce awesome websites.
I also have skills in WordPress, Digital Design, Public Speaking, Copy Writing and Blogging.
