Liz Eeles: An author's website

I worked closely with Liz over an extended period of time to build a simple website to promote her holiday romance novels and showcase blog posts.


I opted to build the site using WordPress to allow Liz to later update it easily for herself - adding blog posts and new books.
To make it bespoke to Liz's ideas of how she wanted it to look, I adapted an existing theme with a significant amount of custom CSS and JavaScript.
