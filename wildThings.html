Wild Things - A Group Portfolio

This is a group project I worked on at University. We were tasked with creating a portfolio site which showcased the skills of each memeber of our group.

The team included some very creative members with a talent for art, design and animation, so we decided on a theme which would allow us to highlight these skills.
My personal expertise laid more in putting the website together - combining the assets and animations that had been created and writing the copy for the site. I also took on the role of project managing, making sure the assets were created in timely fashion and that everyone knew their role and had the support they needed.
Unfortunately, we didn't have time to perfect the site, there are plenty of things which need improving, but it was a fun project which we are proud to have created.
